Frequently Asked Questions
▶ How long does shipping take?

Rugs typically take 1-2 weeks to create and ship, depending on availability. We always try to deliver ASAP! Shipping within Colombia is FREE, and international shipping times vary by location.

▶ Can I customize my rug design?

Absolutely! For custom pet rugs, visit our Pet Section and upload your photo for a FREE digital proposal within 24 hours. For other custom designs, use our Contact form to discuss your vision.

▶ What payment methods do you accept?

We accept payments through Mercado Pago (Colombia) and PayPal (International). Colombian customers can also use Nequi for convenient local payments.

▶ How do I care for my rug?

Use a vacuum or wet wipes for regular cleaning. DO NOT use a washing machine or expose to direct sunlight. With proper care, your rug will last for years. All rugs include a 1-year warranty!

▶ What are international shipping costs?

Colombia enjoys FREE shipping! For international orders:

Gospel & Wall/Floor Rugs:
• Latin America & US: add $30 USD shipping fee
• Europe & Australia: add $80 USD shipping fee

Keyboard Rugs:
• Latin America & US: add $25 USD shipping fee
• Europe & Australia: add $50 USD shipping fee

▶ Do you offer refunds or exchanges?

We stand behind our quality! If there's an issue with your rug, contact us within 7 days of delivery. All rugs come with a 1-year warranty covering manufacturing defects.

▶ What makes WPAP rugs special?

WPAP (Wedha's Pop Art Portrait) is a unique geometric art style featuring bold colors and angular patterns. Each rug is hand-tufted with premium acrylic yarn, featuring non-slip backing and carved 3D details for texture and depth.

▶ Can I see a preview before ordering?

Yes! For custom pet rugs, we provide a FREE digital mockup within 24 hours of submission. Once you approve the design, we require a deposit to begin crafting your masterpiece. For Gospel, Keyboard, or Wall/Floor rugs, what you see in our gallery is what you get!
